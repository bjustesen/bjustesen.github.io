The Weather

We speak about the weather almost every day. Before we begin doing something, we want to know what the weather will be like.

Why do we speak about the weather so often? The answer is not very difficult. The weather in Great Britain is very changeable. Of course we want sunny weather when we go for a walk in the country or when we play games outside. When the day is rainy, we stay at home for the whole day, which can be very boring for many people.

When we are at the seaside, windy weather is not enjoyable. It’s difficult to swim when the wind blows. And the wind can change good weather into bad. The sun will stop shining, and the rain may come.

And is there something which we can do in any weather? Yes, of course, there is. As English people say, “rain or shine” (=in any weather) we can study. Leaning is good in any weather, isn’t it?
