Alignment

Amazon

amazon.com

I believe that this screen shot exemplifies alignmnet because all the boxes are lined up nicely. The entierity of amazon has these little squares that are all aligned perfectly with one another. Alignment is defined as "arrangement in a straight line, or in correct or appropriate releivtve positions" I believe that Amazon.com displays this perfectly with their many boxes and panels.

Contrast

On the Grid

onthegird.city

I believe that this screen shot exemplifies contrast because contrast is defined as " the state of being strikingly different from something else." I think that the blue and white create a large contrast and make the website pop in its own way.

Proximity

Church of Jesus Christ

churchofjesuschrist.org

I believe that this screen shot exemplifies proximity because all proximity is defined as "nearness in space..." I think that the Church of Jesus Christ website has a good amount of proximity between it's different features. In this screenshot we see the links above a their spacing from the pictures below. It gives us the feeling and knowledge that the two items are seperate but related.
